# Artificial Intelligence Basics

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of building machines capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence.

## Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subset of AI where algorithms learn patterns from data.

### Deep Learning

Deep Learning uses neural networks with many layers.

- Chatbots
- Recommendation Systems
- Autonomous Vehicles
- Medical Diagnosis
