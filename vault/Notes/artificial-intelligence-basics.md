## Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) involves creating machines that mimic human intelligence. Machine Learning and Deep Learning are subsets of AI, with applications in chatbots, recommendation systems, and autonomous vehicles.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of building machines that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence. Machine Learning is a subset of AI where algorithms learn patterns from data.
